The Number Six Club

Located right in the center of Cambridge just off the MIT campus, the Number Six Club offers a great and convinient summer location to live in. Just a few minutes walk from Boston and Cambridge. Some of the rooms overlook the picturesque view of the Charles River that is quite a sight to behold in the lovely Boston summer. It is quite close to the heart of the city, but still offers a peaceful environment.

Our house has an open kitchen complete with oven and fridge space, coin-operated laundry machines, sun deck and BBQ in garden, and spacious common areas. We can offer high-speed Wi-Fi Internet with static IP addresses and there are house computers for everyone to use.

Welcome to House.us!

Many students reside in the greater Boston area every summer. During these twelve weeks, they need temporary accommodation in a city with a competitive housing market. Universities designate few dorms during the summer for undergraduates, reserving other dorms for conferences, youth camps, and renovations. Luckily, House.us helps students and interns with unlocking the doors of the MIT fraternities, sororities and living groups for the summer.

Below is an overview of the MIT fraternities, sororities and living groups we serve. By pressing each fraternity on the map, you can also see more details about the given fraternity. Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions.
