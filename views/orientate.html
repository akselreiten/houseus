The Number Six Club

Located right in the center of Cambridge just off the MIT campus, the Number Six Club offers a great and convinient summer location to live in. Just a few minutes walk from Boston and Cambridge. Some of the rooms overlook the picturesque view of the Charles River that is quite a sight to behold in the lovely Boston summer. It is quite close to the heart of the city, but still offers a peaceful environment.

Our house has an open kitchen complete with oven and fridge space, coin-operated laundry machines, sun deck and BBQ in garden, and spacious common areas. We can offer high-speed Wi-Fi Internet with static IP addresses and there are house computers for everyone to use.

Delta Tau Delta

The Delta Tau Delta fraternity house is a beautiful brownstone located in Boston's Back Bay at 416 Beacon Street. The great location provides easy access to the Prudential Center, Boston Commons, Fenway Park, Kenmore and Hynes Convention Center T stops, and MIT.

Housing comes with complete access to all the amenities of 416 Beacon Street. We have a TV room with a 65-inch TV, pool table, and piano. There is a ping pong table and bar downstairs. We have a fully equipped kitchen and laundry room. Rooms are furnished with couches, mattresses, desks, dressers, and closet space. All rooms have high speed internet and the option of DirecTV. Roofdeck equipped with tables, chairs, couches, a hammock, and a grill.

Welcome to House.us!

Many students reside in the greater Boston area every summer. During these twelve weeks, they need temporary accommodation in a city with a competitive housing market. Universities designate few dorms during the summer for undergraduates, reserving other dorms for conferences, youth camps, and renovations. House.us provides a new way to search for summer housing in Boston, and makes the whole process easier and less time consuming.

At our website, you can find all MIT fraternities, sororities and living groups we serve. Explore the map to learn more about where they are located and what kind of housing they offer.Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions.
